Wah Fu Estate: Heritage Web Experience

A bilingual-friendly editorial web experience about Wah Fu Estate that presents a Hong Kong public-housing community through a full-bleed visual hero, quick overview cards, a discovery slider, feature-story cards, historical timeline content, and practical visitor guidance.
